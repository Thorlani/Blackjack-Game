Quick Rules About the Game
1. The game of Blackjack is a game of luck in which to win you have to pick a card that amounts to the sum of 21.
2. However, if you pick a card whose sum is less than 21 you will not win but you will be given the opportunity to pick another card.
3. Nevertheless, if the sum of your card is more than 21, you have lost the game.
4. Make sure to click the RESTART BUTTON after each card pick.
